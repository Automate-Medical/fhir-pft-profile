KCO

A measurement of a carbon monoxide transfer coefficient, in mL/min/mmHg.

Also referred to as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Post-bronchodilator

Carbon monoxide transfer coefficient, post-bronchodilator. Note: also known as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Post-bronchodilator Percent Of Predicted

Carbon monoxide transfer coefficient (% predicted), post-bronchodilator. Note: also known as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Post-bronchodilator Zscore

Carbon monoxide transfer coefficient post-bronchodilator z-score. Note: also known as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Pre-bronchodilator

A measurement of carbon monoxide transfer coefficient pre-bronchodilation, in mL/min/mmHg.

Also referred to as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Pre-bronchodilator Percent Of Predicted

Carbon monoxide transfer coefficient (% predicted), pre-bronchodilator. Note: also known as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Pre-bronchodilator Zscore

Carbon monoxide transfer coefficient pre-bronchodilator z-score. Note: also known as KCO or DLCO/VA.
KCO Percent Of Predicted

A measurement of carbon monoxide transfer coefficient as the percentage of some predicted reference value.
KCO Zscore

The z-score of a carbon monoxide transfer coefficient measurement, calculated from some reference distribution.
Pulmonary Function Test (PFT) Diagnostic Report

Diagnostic report for a pulmonary function test.
SpO2 at rest

A measurement of oxygen saturation in arterial blood taken by pulse oximetry, with the patient at rest.
Total Lung Capacity By Helium Single Breath (TLC_sb)

A measurement of total lung capacity by helium, single breath, in liters.
VI/VC

Inspired volume (V_I) as a percentage of vital capacity (V_C).
